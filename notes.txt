All objects should have:
    __init__
    __repr__
    __str__

Other useful methods:
    __itr__
    __len__
    

Linked List methods:
    append(element)
    clear()
    copy()
    count(element)
    extend(list)
    index(element)
    insert(pos, element)
    pop(pos)
    remove(element)
    reverse()
    sort()

Stack methods:
    empty()
    size()
    top()
    push(element)
    pop()

Queue methods:
    enqueue(element)
    dequeue()
    front()
    rear()

BST methods:
    insert(element)
    remove(element)
    get_min()
    get_max()
    exists(element)
    inorder()
    preorder()
    postorder()
